Activities to Do in Siliguri

  • siliguri
Post By Mehek Verma
Published May 30, 2025

If you’re in Siliguri and wondering how to make your day more fun or not sure what to do in your free time, this list is just for you. Whether you want to chill at a cafe, go shopping, explore nature, or do something meaningful—– there’ is a lot you can try out here.

This blog brings you simple ideas and places to check out—perfect for a weekend plan, a solo day out, or showing someone around town.

Here are some top things to do in and around Siliguri to spend your time:-

Visit Off-beat Places

If you want to spend time in nature, away from the usual crowd, these offbeat places near Siliguri are perfect. You can go for a short drive or hike, take photos, and enjoy some peaceful and quiet time in nature.

Some of these places are as follows:-

  • Geetkhola– This is a calm riverside spot with lush greenery- great for picnics and walks.
  • Rainbow Waterfalls– It is a peaceful waterfall near Jorpokhri, perfect for a short trek.
  • Muktikhola– It’s a beautiful place surrounded by forests, this river spot is great for offbeat day trips.
  • Tonglu– A small mountain village where you can view stunning peaks of Mt. Kanchenjunga.
  • Dawaipani– It is a quiet village near Darjeeling which is ideal for a peaceful getaway.

Rent a Ride & Travel

If you want to explore Siliguri and nearby areas on your own terms, then what else could be a better option than renting a bike or car. It gives you the freedom and flexibility, which is perfect for road trips with friends and family.

Some popular rental options are:-

  • Rent2Rider
  • Rentrip
  • Royal Riders
  • Makemytrip
  • Savaari
  • Zoom

Explore Good Cafes

Cafe culture in Siliguri has grown a lot in recent years and now Siliguri is a home to a good number of cafes. With cozy interiors, varied cuisines, and aesthetic vibes, these cafes make perfect hangout spots. Some of the famous cafes in Siliguri are:

  • The April Cafe
  • P’s Holiday Bistro
  • Backyard Bistro
  • Cafe Pai
  • Two and a Half Mile
  • Kafena Cafe
  • Artoli Coffee & Eatery

Visit Amusement & Safari Parks

If you are looking for some fun, adventure and joy, then, there are some amusement parks in Siliguri that are ideal for families, kids and adults.

Dreamland Amusement Park is a good option for fun rides and games.
Opening Hours: 10 a.m. to 6.30 p.m.
Website: https://dreamlandfunworld.in/
Address: NearFulbari Ghoshpukur Canal Road, Rahamu, West Bengal

Savin Kingdom is another great place for arcade games & water rides.
Opening Hours: 10 a.m. to 6 p.m.
Website: https://www.savinkingdom.com/
Address: Darjeeling More, behind Baba Loknath Temple, Dagapur, Siliguri

Bengal Safari is a great place if you want to explore and experience wildlife and nature, as it offers safari rides and fun activities like zipline, golf cart rides, toy train ride and wall climbing.
Opening Hours: 9 a.m. to 5 p.m.
Website: https://www.northbengalwildanimalspark.in/
Address: 5th Mile, Sevoke Road, Salugara, Siliguri

Go Shopping

If shopping is your therapy, then Siliguri has lots of places from branded stores in malls to street markets with budget finds.

Local Markets– If you are looking to go for some budget shopping and enjoy street food, then here are some options for you:

  • Bidhan Market
  • Mahabirsthan market
  • Hong Kong Market

Malls– If you like to shop from branded stores, then going to malls is a great option, also you can enjoy good food and watch movies there.

  • City Centre
  • Vega Circle
  • Acropolis
  • Cosmos

Explore Art & Culture

Siliguri has a rich mix of tradition from monasteries and temples to local art shows and heritage rides. So, if you want to explore the cultural side of Siliguri, then you cannot miss the following places:

Joy Ride/Toy Train Ride: Joy Ride from Darjeeling to Ghum (UNESCO heritage). Tickets can be booked online through IRCTC or at Siliguri junction.

Temples:

  • Iskon Mandir 📍
  • Pranami Temple 📍
  • Sevokeshwari Kali Mandir 📍
  • Shyam Mandir 📍

Monasteries:

  • Evam Monastery 📍
  • Salugara Monastery 📍

Ramkinkar Art Gallery: Hosts local art exhibitions and cultural events. 📍

Visit Libraries

If you enjoy reading or want a quiet place to study, then public libraries are a great way to spend time. Peaceful, free, and perfect for spending a few focused hours.

  • Additional District Library, Hakimpara-Huge collection and study-friendly space.
  • Bangiya Sahitya Parishad Sub-divisional Library, College Para- Good for Bengali literature and academic texts.
  • Shaktigarh Pathagar, loacated near Pradip Nagar, Shaktigarh is another public library that you can visit.

Become a Volunteer

If you want to spend time doing something meaningful, then you can volunteer at these NGOs. This is a great opportunity to meet like-minded people, and gain real experience in different fields—like animal care, youth empowerment, or the environment.

Human Welfare:

  • Robin hood Army Siliguri
  • Aadiya Youth Foundation
  • Siliguri Unique Social Welfare Society
  • Siliguri Terai Lions Blood Bank
  • Siliguri Subhaspally Welfare Organisation

Animal Welfare:

  • Peace Animals Paradise Association
  • Nirvana Animal Welfare

Environment:

  • Grow Billion Trees Foundation
  • Save Earth for Life
  • Himalayan Nature & Adventure Foundation

Turns out, Siliguri has more to offer than we usually think. From cool hangout spots to peaceful escapes and even meaningful ways to spend your time—it’s all there.
Save this blog for future plans, and feel free to share it with anyone new in town or looking for ideas. Happy Exploring!
