List of Government Schools in Siliguri

  • Blog
  • siliguri
Post By braand
Published December 20, 2025

Siliguri has many government schools and many students study in these schools. These schools are important because education cost is low and teachers are experienced. Government schools are mostly run by the Education Department. Parents send their children here for regular studies and discipline. Some schools are very old and people know them for many years.

Below are some famousgovernment schools in Siliguri with important details.

Haiderpara Buddha Bharti High School

Haiderpara Buddha Bharti High School was started in 1984. It is a co-education school, so boys and girls both study here. Teaching is done in Bengali language. The school has classes from Class 5 to Class 10. The building is simple and not very modern. Students sit in normal classrooms. There is a library but it is small. Playground is there and students play during games time. Computer class is also there but only basic things are taught.

Classes: Class 5 to 10
Category: Co-Ed
Address: Haiderpara Main Road, Ward 40, Haidar Para, Siliguri

Nabin Pathmandir

Nabin Pathmandir is a government primary school. It was started in the year 1972. This school is for both boys and girls. Teaching language is Bengali. The school has classes from Class 1 to Class 4 and also a pre-primary section. Many small children from nearby areas come here. Mid-day meal is given to students daily. Teachers mainly teach basic reading, writing and maths so students can learn slowly.

Classes: Class 1 to 4
Category: Co-Ed
Address: Rathkhola Road, Subhas Pally, Siliguri

Siliguri Boys High School

Siliguri Boys High School is one of the oldest schools in Siliguri. It started in 1918. This school is only for boys students. Bengali is the teaching medium. Classes are from Class 5 to Class 12. Students can take Arts, Science or Commerce in higher classes. The school has many classrooms and a big playground. Library and computer room are also there. Mid-day meal is given. Many students have passed from this school.

Classes: Class 5 to 12
Category: Boys
Address: College Para, Siliguri

Siliguri Girls High School

Siliguri Girls High School was started in 1946. This school is only for girls. Classes are from Class 5 to Class 12. Teaching is done in Bengali language. The school is known for discipline. Classrooms are simple. Library and computer room is available. Mid-day meal is given to students. Parents trust this school because it is old and well known.

Classes: Class 5 to 12
Category: Girls
Address: Ward 17, Ashutosh Mukherjee Rd, College Para, Old Matigara, Siliguri

Nilnalini Vidyamandir

Nilnalini Vidyamandir started in 1953. It is a co-education government school. Bengali is the teaching medium. Classes are from Class 5 to Class 12. The school building is quite big. There are many classrooms and staff rooms. Playground is also there. Students use library and computer room sometimes. Mid-day meal is given regularly. Teachers focus more on exams and syllabus.

Classes: Class 5 to 12
Category: Co-Ed
Address: Ramkrishna Road, Ashram Para, Near Pani Tanki More (Clock Tower), Siliguri

Siliguri Baradkanta Vidyapath

Siliguri Baradkanta Vidyapath was started in 1960. It is a co-education school. Teaching is in Bengali medium. Classes are from Class 5 to Class 12. Arts, Science and Commerce streams are available. The school has classrooms, library and playground. Mid-day meal is provided. Many students from local area study here.

Classes: Class 5 to 12
Category: Co-Ed
Address: Rabindra Sarani, Rathkhola, Subhas Pally, Siliguri

Hakimpara Ballika Vidyalaya

Hakimpara Ballika Vidyalaya was established in 1958. It is a government school only for girls. The school has classes from Class 5 to Class 12. Teaching language is Bengali. There are classrooms and a library. Computer classes are also there but limited. Mid-day meal is given. Many girls study here from nearby areas.

Classes: Class 5 to 12
Category: Girls
Address: Siliguri College Campus Road, Ward 17, Hakim Para, Siliguri

Siliguri Vidyasagar Vidyalaya

Siliguri Vidyasagar Vidyalaya started in 1978. It is a co-education school. Bengali is used for teaching. Classes are from Class 5 to Class 10. Mid-day meal is given to students. The school mainly focuses on regular classes and exams. Facilities are simple.

Classes: Class 5 to 10
Category: Co-Ed
Address: Vidya Sagar Road, Ward 7, Vidya Sagar Pally, Khalpara, Siliguri

Siliguri Krishnamaya Memorial Nepali High School

This school was started in 1950. It is a co-education school. Teaching language is Nepali. Classes are from Class 1 to Class 4. Many Nepali speaking students come here. Mid-day meal is given to students. The school helps students study in their own language.

Classes: Class 1 to 4
Category: Co-Ed
Address: Ashrampara, Rabindra Sarani, Siliguri

Bharati Hindi Vidyalaya

Bharati Hindi Vidyalaya was started in 1967. It is a Hindi medium government school. Boys and girls both study here. Classes are from Class 5 to Class 12. The school has classrooms and a library. Computer is used for teaching sometimes. Mid-day meal is provided. Many Hindi speaking families send their children here.

Classes: Class 5 to 12
Category: Co-Ed
Address: Old Matigara Rd, Ward 47, Patiram Jote, Siliguri

Government schools in Siliguri are very helpful for many families. They provide education at low cost and teaching is regular even if facilities are simple. Many students study here and finish their education successfully. For parents who want other options, private schools like CBSE Schools and ICSE Schools are also in Siliguri, offering different curriculum and extra activities. Still, government schools remain important and continue to help children in the city get education.
