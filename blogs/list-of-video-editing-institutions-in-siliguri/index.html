List of Video Editing Institutions in Siliguri

  • siliguri

Introduction

One of the most fun things you can learn these days is how to edit videos. Video editors are in high demand for anything from making YouTube videos to Instagram reels and short films. This site will help you find the best video editing institutes in Siliguri if you live in North Bengal.

This site gives a clear and simple list of video editing institutes in Siliguri where students, beginners, and individuals who already work can learn how to edit videos step by step. It’s very crucial to pick the correct place to learn how to edit videos in Siliguri, whether you want to do it for a living or just for your own projects.

Some of the well known institutes in Siliguri that offer professional video editing training are listed below:

Arena Animation, Siliguri

Arena Animation is one of the most well-known schools in Siliguri for creative media and entertainment. The institute is close to the Siliguri College gate and draws in students who want to make media. The school has certificate programs in making digital material like video editing, animation, VFX, and more.

Courses offered: The institute offers certificate courses in digital content development, video editing, animation, and VFX.

Fees: The cost of a short-term certificate course is 4k while the cost of a professional diploma is around 3 Lakhs

MAAC Siliguri

MAAC Siliguri is part of the Maya Academy of Advanced Creative (MAAC) network and was founded in 2007. The school teaches students both theory and practice, so they are ready for the job market. The school also helps students get internships and jobs with well-known brands.

Courses offered: MAAC provides numerous courses, including Graphic Design, 3D Animation, VFX, Gaming Design, Video Editing, and Flimmaking.

Fees: The prices depend on the course. For short-term courses, they are normally under 7,000, while for professional degrees, they are usually between 90,000 and 3.8 lakhs.

The school is well-known in Siliguri for its professional training, experienced teachers, and innovative media courses. The school has a lot of different classes in animation, VFX, graphic design, and video editing.

Moople – Institute of Animation

Moople is well-known in the realm of creative multimedia and design schools all around the world. The institute wants to train students who want to work in creative disciplines such animation, visual effects, motion graphics, video editing, and digital inventions. The institute has degree and diploma programs that include hands-on work.

Courses offered: The school provides certificate and degree programs in digital marketing, web design, 3D gaming, animation, graphic design, and VFX.

Fees: Certificate courses cost between 35,000 and 2.3 lakhs, while degree programs cost between 3.85 and 4.3 lakhs.

There are many courses at the institute, including those in digital marketing, graphic design, branding, video editing, and UI/UX design. They also help with internships and job placement.

Inspiria Knowledge Campus

Inspiria features a wide range of courses, including a video editing course as part of its 4-year B.Sc. in Multimedia, Animation, and Graphic Design program. The college offers both academic and practical learning, as well as internships. The college’s goal is to help students who want to work in design and the arts find the right path.

Courses offered: The college provides a four-year B.Sc. in Multimedia, Animation, and Graphic Design.

Exposure – The School of Photography

Exposure is a well-known photography and videography school in Siliguri that wants to give its pupils a creative education. The school has diploma and master’s degree programs that you can do online or in person. They also hosted workshops, contests, and other events to help kids choose a career with the help of professionals.

Courses offered: The school offers diplomas in a lot of different areas, such as photography, graphic design, wildlife photography, video editing, and cinematography.

Conclusion

Learning how to edit videos can give you a lot of new chances in the future. It can help you acquire a job, start freelancing, increase your YouTube channel, or even establish your own creative career.

We hope this list of video editing schools in Siliguri helps you choose the best location to learn. Always look into the course content, trainers, practical classes, and student assistance before attending any school.

Choose the video editing school in Siliguri that fits your ambitions and start your adventure today if you really want to study. Anyone can become a confident video editor if they practice often and get the correct advice.
