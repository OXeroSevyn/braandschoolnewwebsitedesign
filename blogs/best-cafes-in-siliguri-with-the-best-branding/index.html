Best Cafes in Siliguri- In terms of Branding

  • siliguri
Post By braand
Published October 23, 2024

The café culture in Siliguri has grown significantly in recent years, with a few standout places known not only for their mouthwatering food and excellent service but also for their unique stories, ambiance, and branding.

Here are Some Cafes with Impressive Branding.

1. P’s Holiday Bistro

P’s Holiday Bistro is an art cafe located in Siliguri; it is one of a kind in the town and serves both good food and a great ambiance. It’s a perfect place for food lovers , art, and music enthusiast.

Rating: 4.8
Visiting Hours: 12:00 pm to 10:00 pm (Closed on Tuesdays)
Address: Ice Factory Rd, Ward 46, Champasari, Siliguri

The “P’s Holiday Bistro” logo has a clean, elegant look with soft green tones. A flower on the “P” and a sun symbol suggest a nature-inspired, warm, aesthetic and welcoming vibe. The mix of serif and cursive fonts adds both sophistication and casual charm, ideal for a cozy, fresh dining experience.

P’s Holiday Bistro has a visually appealing profile with a focus on art and creativity. The posts showcase their food, art events, and the cafe’s ambiance. The profile’s overall aesthetic is inviting and engaging.

2. Backyard Bistro

Backyard Bistro isn’t just a cafe it’s your new favorite hangout place with two spots in the city! With cozy outdoor seating, a killer vibe, and a menu so packed it could satisfy even the pickiest eater, this place is a no-brainer for anyone craving great food and good times. Trust us, your taste buds will thank you!

Rating: 4.1
Visiting Hours: 9:00 am to 11:00 pm
Address: Sarkar Villa, 20, Birendra Krishna Bhadra Sarani, near CBI office, College Para, Hakim Para, Siliguri

The “Backyard Bistro” logo has a fresh, natural vibe with green tones and leaf accents. The bold “BACKYARD” in the center contrasts with softer, cursive fonts for “the” and “bistro,” creating a balanced mix of casual and modern. The circular design with leaves suggests a cozy, outdoor-inspired dining experience.

Backyard Bistro has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their cafe’s serene ambiance and tasty food. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the beautiful surroundings. The overall aesthetic is inviting and relaxing.

3. Dakini Cafe

You should visit Dakini Cafe if you basically live for Italian cuisine and culture!With its warm, classy decor and that oh-so-perfect pizza and coffee combo, this spot feels like a little slice of Italy nestled right in Siliguri. Whether you’re here to savor a latte or devour a pizza (or both, because why not?), this is the place to indulge all your Italian cravings!


Rating: 4.4
Visiting Hours: 11:30 am to 9:00 pm
Address:  BSF Rd, Ward 42, Salugara, Siliguri

The “Dakini Cafe” logo has a sleek, minimalist design with white text on a solid black circle. The modern, bold font for “DAKINI CAFE” contrasts with smaller, refined lettering for “Pizzeria & Bakery,” giving it a sophisticated yet straightforward look. It suggests a stylish, contemporary spot for pizza and baked goods.

Dakini Cafe has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their Italian cuisine and cozy atmosphere. The posts showcase their delicious dishes, the cafe’s ambiance, and special events. The overall aesthetic is inviting and appetizing.

4. Cafe Andante

It is a Korean cafe and restaurant serving Korean food, desserts and coffee making it an ideal hangout place with a soft touch of plant decor.You’ll spot a piece of Korea in Siliguri! Step into a cozy cafe and restaurant and experience the delightful flavours of Korea, complete with delicious food, sweet treats, aromatic coffee, and soothing plant decor.


Rating: 4.4
Visiting Hours:11:00 am to 9:00 pm (Closed on Tuesdays)
Address: Taps and tiles building, opposite of Kali mandir, Kadamtala, Champasari More Rd, Siliguri

The logo for Cafe Andante is a simple, circular design with the word “Andante” written in a cursive font. The words “Coffee & Studio” and “Est 2019” are also included in smaller font. The logo is set against a coral-red background.The Andante logo is a simple, elegant, and memorable design that effectively conveys the cafe’s brand identity.

Cafe Andante has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their authentic Korean cuisine and coffee. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s ambiance.

5. Cafe Pai

Pai cafe is one of those great looking aesthetic cafes that have all soft pink indoors with a mix of Italian Korean Japanese and a bit of continental cuisine.If you’re into stylish, aesthetic vibes, Pai Café is the place for you! With its soft pink interior and delicious mix of Italian, Korean, Japanese, and Continental cuisine, it’s the perfect spot for a coffee and a bite to eat.

Rating: 4.2
Visiting Hours:12:00 pm to 9:30 pm
Address: Hotel Dolly Pvt. Ltd, Bidhan Road, Nazrul Sarani, Near Pani Tanki More (Clock Tower), Siliguri

The Café Pai logo features a circular frame with decorative flourishes and the words “CAFÉ” and “Pai” in different styles. The word “Pai” is written in a cursive font, adding a touch of elegance. The overall design is classic and timeless.

Café Pai has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their cafe’s ambiance and food. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s interior.

6. Olive Tree

Olive Tree is a cafe cum bakery specializing in European cuisine. This cafe has a green and white theme giving a fresh, peaceful and natural dining experience where you can enjoy coffee with mouthwatering
pastries while experiencing the cozy ambiance.

Rating: 4.4
Visiting Hours: 8:00 am to 10:00 pm
Address: Ground Floor, PRM Tower, Sevoke Rd, opposite Suchitra Hotel, Ward 43, Dasrath Pally, Bhanu Nagar, Siliguri

The logo features an olive branch with olives, set within a circular frame. The olive branch symbolizes peace, abundance, and prosperity, while the green background enhances the natural feel.

Olive Tree has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their bakery and cozy atmosphere. The posts showcase their delicious cakes, pastries, and the cafe’s ambiance.

7. The Wood Apple Cafe & Grill

This pet friendly cafe has wooden decor and serves North Indian, Chinese, fast food, continental, Sichuan and other cuisines making a perfect meal for all taste buds.

Rating: 4.2
Visiting Hours: 12:00 pm to 10:00 pm
Address:  Sonartori Village, Patel Road, Ward 2, Pradhan Nagar, Siliguri 

The Wood Apple logo features a minimalist design with a person sitting at a table in a circular frame. The color palette is warm and inviting, with a brown figure against a beige background. The cursive font for “The Wood Apple” adds a touch of elegance, while the simple icon effectively conveys the cafe’s atmosphere. The overall design is clean, modern, and memorable.

The Woodapple Cafe & Grill has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their cozy atmosphere and delicious food. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s ambiance. The overall aesthetic is inviting and appetizing.

8. Coffee D’ Costa

Serving a variety of flavored coffees, shakes, and other beverages, along with fast food and desserts, the place is very warm and inviting with soft jazz, a rustic decor, and a very stylish ambience. Coffee D’Costa is definitely worth a visit.

Rating: 4.5
Visiting Hours: 11:30 am to 11:00 pm
Address: Millenium Towers, Sevoke Road Near Bazaar Kolkata, opp. LIC building, Siliguri

The Coffee D’Costa logo is a unique and memorable design that combines a vintage aesthetic with a playful touch. The coffee cup, hat, glasses, and mustache create a whimsical and friendly character. The color scheme is simple and effective, with black and white for the main elements and a teal green for the text. The overall design is eye-catching and instantly recognizable, making it a strong representation of the Coffee D’Costa brand.

Coffee D’Costa has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their coffee and community. The posts showcase their coffee drinks, special events, and the cafe’s ambiance.

9. Two and A Half Mile

It is a modern cafe with quirky yet subtle pastel aesthetics, creating a calm and inviting atmosphere. This cafe specialize in offering in continental cuisine and desserts, making it an ideal place to be on your list to relax and enjoy some peace while having tasty treats.

Rating: 4.5
Visiting Hours: 10:30 am to 10:00 pm
Address: Checkpost, near Bhakti Nagar Police Station, Ward 42, Don Bosco Colony, Siliguri

The Two and a Half Mile logo features the number “2 1/2” in a bold, playful font, set within a half-circle shape. The word “mile” is placed below, completing the design. The logo’s clean lines and vibrant color make it eye-catching and memorable.

Two and a Half Mile has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their modern cafe experience. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s ambiance.

10. Gupshup Cafe

This cafe opened recently and offers a homely environment and delicious food, with a range of fast food and beverages and delightful evening snacking options to satisfy every craving.

Rating: 5
Visiting Hours: 10:00 am to 10:00 pm (Closed on Mondays)
Address: Ward no 41, Ulka club, Jyoti Nagar, Bankim Nagar, Siliguri

The Gupshup logo features the word “Gupshup” in a stylized font, with a small bowl icon above it. The phrase “cafe by mithis” and “drop the tea” are included below, adding a playful and inviting tone. The logo is set against a dark green background, creating a clean and modern look.

Gupshup Cafe has a vibrant profile with a focus on their cozy atmosphere and diverse menu. The posts showcase their food, drinks, and special events. The overall aesthetic is inviting and energetic.

11. Home Cafe

With cute decor and quite a variety of Nepalese, Chinese and continental cuisine – it can be defined as one of the best cafes in Siliguri. It’s the perfect spot to enjoy a meal with friends and family.

Rating: 4.3
Visiting Hours: 10:30 am to 3:30 pm
Address: Block 2, Dhan Singh Thapa Path, Near SIT College, Methibari, Salbari, Patiram Jote, Siliguri

The Home Cafe logo features a plate, fork, and spoon, symbolizing food and dining. The words “Home Cafe” are written in a playful font, and the phrase “Made with Love” is added below, conveying warmth and care. The logo’s simple design and positive message make it inviting and memorable.

Home Cafe has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their homemade Nepalese, Chinese, and Continental cuisine. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s ambiance. The overall aesthetic is inviting and appetizing.

12. Kafena Cafe

It is one of the most pinterest-worthy cafes in the town with indoor, outdoor and rooftop settings offering Korean, Tibetan and continental cuisine, with Japanese vibes and beautiful environment.

Rating: 4.2
Visiting Hours: 12:00 pm to 10:00 pm
Address:  Near Salugara Monastery, Near Golden gate , Debi Chaudhurani Rd, near golden gate, Salugara, Siliguri

The Kafena Café logo is a visually appealing design that effectively represents the brand’s identity. The coffee cup, beans, and leaves create a warm and inviting image, associated with the cafe’s offerings. The circular design adds a touch of elegance and simplicity, making the logo easy to remember. The color scheme is soft and inviting, with a beige background and brown, green, and black accents. Overall, the logo is a well-crafted and memorable representation of the Kafena Café brand.

Kafena Cafe has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their Korean, Tibetan, and Continental cuisine. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s ambiance.

13. Berlin Coffeehouse

This cafe has a nice European- themed calm ambience, offering Continental food and a good variety of beverages. It’s one of the best spots to unwind and enjoy a mouthwatering meal.

Rating: 4.2
Visiting Hours: 11:00 am to 10:00 pm
Address: ML Acropolis, Ward Number 13, Bankim Nagar, Siliguri

The logo for Berlin Coffeehouse features the word “Berlin” in a stylized script, accompanied by a simple outline of a coffee cup with steam rising from it. The overall design is minimalist and elegant, suggesting a sophisticated and welcoming atmosphere.

Berlin Coffeehouse has a visually appealing profile with a focus on their coffee, food, and music. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s ambiance.

14. Cafe Karmaa Diaries

Karmaa Diaries is the first ever mindfulness cafe in Siliguri where you can enjoy delicious food together with live band performances in a soothing friendly environment. With live music and thoughful affirmations, each dish is a treat for both body and soul.

Rating: 4.6
Visiting Hours: 11:00 am to 11:00 pm
Address: Siddhi Arcade, Upper Bhanu Nagar, Ward 4, Dasrath Pally, Bhanu Nagar, Siliguri

The logo for Karmaa Diaries features the word “Karmaa” in a playful, handwritten style, with a boomerang symbol incorporated into the “K.” The tagline “Hope faith leap” is included below, and the overall design suggests a welcoming and inclusive atmosphere.

Karmaa Diaries has a visually appealing profile with a focus on mindfulness and community. The posts showcase their events, workshops, and the cafe’s ambiance. The overall aesthetic is calming and inviting.

15. Artoli Coffee & Eatery

Artoli cafe is a beautiful and peaceful hangout go-to spot for great food and ambiance in siliguri.

Rating: 3.2
Visiting Hours: 11:00 am to 10:00 pm
Address: 24/1, Gaushala Road, Ward 26, Babupara, Siliguri

The Artoli Coffee & Eatery logo features the word “Artoli” in a stylized script, enclosed in a circular border. The tagline “Coffee & Eatery” is included below, and the overall design suggests a welcoming and inviting atmosphere.

Artoli Coffee & Eatery has a vibrant profile with a focus on their delicious food and ambiance. The posts showcase their menu items, special events, and the cafe’s atmosphere. The overall aesthetic is inviting and energetic.

16. K-afé Clover

K-afe Clover is one of Siliguri’s most aesthetically pleasing cafes, offering the perfect blend of cozy ambience, flavourful food, and a warm, inviting vibe. Clover has quickly become a favourite for dates, chill evenings, and unplanned hangouts, where you can enjoy your quiet solo breaks or get some work done. 

Rating: 4.5
Visiting Hours: 11:00 am to 10:00 pm
Address: Old SNT Godown Road, Opp. Sant Nirankari Bhawan, Pradhan Nagar, Siliguri

The K-afe Clover logo features the word “K-AFE CLOVER” in a minimalistic way with the clover in the middle. The logo itself represents the calm and minimal aesthetic, being the overall vibe of the cafe. 

K-afe Clover has a vibrant profile with a focus on cozy corners, warm-toned café shots, and mouth-watering food photography. The post showcases their menu, events and calm aesthetics of the cafe. The overall aesthetic is warm and inviting.

Now, the culture of cafes has bloomed in Siliguri with unique brand concepts that offer not only fantastic food but also marvellous drinks. Each café improves the dining experience through thoughtful design, decor, and ambiance. Many have outdoor elements in green tones to complement their artistically bohemian atmosphere, as in P’s Holiday Bistro and Backyard Bistro. Others, such as Cafe Andante and Dakini Cafe, are a bit more minimalist in giving understated branding for a more sophisticated look.

For the food enthusiast, Cafe Pai is chic pink colored, while South Korea comes to town in Cafe Andante, and thematic spots like Kafena Cafe and Berlin Coffeehouse set calm, inviting spaces that match their cuisine.

Other brand creations go beyond just food such as mindfulness with Karmaa Diaries bringing nurturing with food, music, and affirmations.

Thus, in a nutshell, these are more than just eating establishments because the distinct branding and focus on its interior design continue developing the ambiance at every location to not only engage the customer to stay for a while but also relax and have fun enjoying their visit.
