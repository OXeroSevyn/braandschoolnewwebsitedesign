Siliguri’s Best Job Oriented Courses in 2025

  • siliguri
Post By Mehek Verma
Published April 2, 2025

Finding the right course that actually leads to a job can feel challenging, especially when so many options are available and the industries keep evolving faster. Whether you are a student figuring out your next step or a professional looking to switch careers, choosing the right course and the right institute can make all the difference.

As Siliguri emerges as a commericial town with lots of booming businesses from different sectors and niches and to fulfill the requirements of the industrial needs the institutes in Siliguri offers a range of job oriented courses that can help you build real skills with right guidance to get you hired faster. In this blog, we’ll walk you through the best job oriented courses in Siliguri based on Siliguri’s job market trends of 2025, so you can make the right decision for your future.

BBA & MBA

Management courses like BBA (Bachelor of Business Administration) and MBA (Master of Business Administration) are elite level courses and are highly regarded in Siliguri, offering in-depth knowledge of business management fundamentals. These programs equip students with essential skills in leadership, finance, marketing, operations, entrepreneurship and other managerial skills.

Institutes Offering Management Courses in Siliguri

  • North Bengal University (BBA & MBA)
  • Siliguri Institute of Technology (BBA & MBA)
  • Salesian College (BBA)
  • Inspiria Knowledge Campus (BBA & MBA)

Career Opportunities

  • Banking & Finance
  • Hospitality & Tourism
  • Retail Management
  • Corporate & Business Management
  • Human Resources Management
  • Entrepreneurship 

Average Salary

BBA Graduates: ₹3 to 4 LPA
MBA Graduates: ₹5 to 10 LPA

Hospitality and Tourism Management

Given Siliguri’s popularity as a tourist destination, hospitality courses are highly relevant for students aspiring to enter the tourism and service industry. These programs provide in-depth knowledge of hotel management, guest services, tourism marketing, and event planning. Additionally, students develop essential skills in management, problem-solving, and adaptability, preparing them for career opportunities in the hospitality sector.

Schools Offering Hospitality Courses in Siliguri

  • IIAS School of Management
  • Inspiria Knowledge Campus
  • International Institute of Hotel Management
  • Siliguri Institute of Technology

Job Opportunities

Hotel & Resort Management – Hotel Manager, Front Office Manager, Food & Beverage Manager
Travel & Tourism Industry – Travel Consultant, Tour Operator, Destination Manager
Event & Wedding Planning – Event Coordinator, Conference Manager
Luxury & Airline Services – Cabin Crew, Customer Service Manager, Hospitality Consultant
Cruise Ship & International Hospitality Roles – Guest Relations Manager, Cruise Director

Average Salary

₹2.5 – 15 lpa

Tea Science

Tea Science is a specialized field focusing on the cultivation, processing, and quality assessment of tea. It also covers tea production management, export, and marketing strategies.

Institutes Offering Tea Science Training in Siliguri

  • Darjeeling Tea Research and Management Association
  • University of North Bengal (Tea Science Department)

Job Opportunities

  • Tea Estate Manager
  • Tea Taster & Quality Analyst
  • Production Supervisor
  • Tea Researcher
  • Tea Export & Marketing Specialist

Average Salary

₹3 – 8 lpa

Law

Integrated law programs combine legal education with another field, such as Arts (BA LLB) or Business Administration (BBA LLB) lasting for 5 years which begin after secondary education. A 3-year LLB program is also available for graduates who have already completed a bachelor’s degree in any field. 

Institutes Offering Integrated Law Programs in Siliguri

  • Siliguri College of Law
  • Indian Institute of Legal Studies

Job Opportunities

  • Litigation & Judiciary – Advocate, Public Prosecutor, Magistrate (after judiciary exams)
  • Corporate Law – Legal Consultant, Corporate Lawyer
  • Government & Public Sector – Legal Advisor, Civil Services (after UPSC or State PCS exams)
  • Legal Research & Academia – Law Professor, Legal Researcher

Average Salary

Entry-level: ₹4 – 6 LPA
Experienced Professionals: ₹8 – 12+ LPA

Interior Designing

Interior Design concentrates on creating usable and beautiful indoor areas. It encompasses space planning, material selection, furniture designing and harmonious blending of art and technical aspects.

Institutes Offering Interior Design Training in Siliguri

  • Inspiria Knowledge Campus
  • INSD (International School of Design)
  • JD Institute of Fashion Technology
  • Cadence Academy

Job Opportunities

Interior designers can work in various industries, including:

  • Architectural & Design Firms – Interior Designer, Space Planner
  • Real Estate & Construction Companies – Project Designer, Property Stylist
  • Freelance & Entrepreneurship – Personal Interior Stylist, Furniture Designer
  • Specialized Fields – Residential Design, Commercial Design, Sustainable Design

Average Salary

₹3-10 lpa

Fashion Designing

Fashion designing is a field of study which involves creating clothes, accessories, and trends by playing with colors, fabrics, and patterns. If you love fashion and creativity, this field lets you express yourself and even start your own brand.

Institutes Offering Fashion Designing Courses in Siliguri

  • INSD
  • JD Institute of Fashion Technology Siliguri
  • NIF Global, Siliguri
  • Inspiria Knowledge Campus
  • Cadence Academy

Job Opportunities

  • Fashion Designer
  • Retail & Merchandising
  • Fashion Journalism & Blogging
  • Textile & Fabric Specialist 
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Consulting & Freelancing 

Average Salary

Entry-level: ₹2.5 – 5 LPA
Experienced Professionals: ₹6 – 12+ LPA

Digital Marketing

Digital marketing programs provide hands-on experience in SEO, SEM, social media marketing, online ads, and content creation. Students learn tools like Google Ads, Facebook Ads, and WordPress to apply their skills in real-world scenarios. This field is growing at a fast rate in Siliguri and across the world.

Institutes Offering Digital Marketing Programs in Siliguri

  • Braand School
  • Internet Marketing School
  • Edugree

Career Opportunities

With businesses shifting online, digital marketers are in high demand across industries like e-commerce, startups, agencies, and corporate marketing teams. Career options include:

  • SEO Specialist
  • Social Media Manager
  • Content Marketer
  • PPC Expert
  • Brand Manager
  • Email Marketer
  • E-commerce Specialist

Average Salary ₹3 – 12+ LPA

Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Data Science

AI and Data Science Professionals are among the hottest fields in demand worldwide and its gaining popularity even in Siliguri from the last few years. These are fields that center around extracting insights from data, building intelligent systems, predictive models, and automating processes.

Institutes Offering AI and Data Science Training in Siliguri

  • Siliguri Institute of Technology
  • Salesian College
  • Inspiria Knowledge Campus

Job Opportunities

  • Data Scientist
  • Machine Learning Engineer
  • AI Researcher
  • Data Analyst
  • Business Intelligence Analyst

Average Salary

₹6 – 15 lpa

UI/UX & Web Development

Creative and technical fields such as UI/UX design and Web development focus on providing excellent and user-oriented interfaces, seamless user experiences, responsive websites, and web applications.

Institutes Offering UI/UX & Web Development Training in Siliguri

  • Arena Animation
  • Moople – Institute of Animation and Design
  • Braand School

Career Opportunities in UI/UX & Web Development

  • UI/UX Designer
  • Front-End Developer
  • Full-Stack Developer
  • Web Designer
  • Product Designer
  • Interaction Designer
  • Mobile App Designer

Average Salary

3 – 10 lpa

Graphic Design

The demand of graphic designers is growing with the rise of digital media. Graphic design programs focus on teaching students the principles of visual communication, color theory, typography, and composition, alongside technical skills in design software like Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign.

Institutes offering Graphic Design Courses  

  • Braand School
  • Arena Animation, Siliguri
  • MAAC
  • INSD

Career Opportunities:

  • Graphic Designer
  • UI Designer
  • Branding Designer
  • Motion Graphics Artist
  • Illustrator
  • Freelance Graphic Designer

Average Salary  

₹3 – 8 lpa

Digital Art

Digital Art is a form of art that is created with the use of computer technologies and / or media. Illustration art, graphics design, animation, concept art are some of the artworks required for industries such as gaming, movie and advertisement.

Institutes Offering Digital Art Training in Siliguri

  • Braand School
  • MAAC Siliguri (Maya Academy of Advanced Cinematics)
  • Arena Animation

Job Opportunities

  • Concept Artist
  • Storyboard Artist
  • Graphic Designer
  • 2D/3D Animator
  • Game Designer
  • Character Designer
  • Freelance Digital Artist

Average Salary

₹2.5 – 9 lpa

Video Editing

As online content becomes more prevalent, the art of video editing is applicable in every field ranging from motion picture and TV production, social media marketing, and even distance learning. This way students acquire storytelling basics, graphic design, color correction, audio post-production and special effects, which are all important when producing high quality videos.

Institutes Offering Video Editing Programs in Siliguri

  • Braand School
  • Arena Animation Siliguri
  • MAAC
  • TGC Animation and Multimedia

Job Opportunities

  • Film Editor
  • Video Editor
  • Motion Graphics Designer
  • VFX Artist
  • Motion Graphics Designer
  • Sound Editor
  • Visual Effects (VFX) Artist
  • Freelance Video Editor

Average Salary

₹2.5 – 12 lpa

Media Science

Media Science is an emerging discipline that concerns itself with the social practices of journalism, advertising, public relations, and new media, and the art of content generation. It covers every aspect in the field of communication such as communicational approach, narrative perspective, and media content creation.

Institutes Offering Media Science Training in Siliguri

  • Inspiria Knowledge Campus, Siliguri

Job Opportunities

  • Journalist
  • Media Planner
  • Content Producer
  • Public Relations Specialist
  • Digital Marketer

Average Salary

₹2.5 – 8+ lpa

Animation and VFX Programs

Animation and VFX artists create and improve animated movies, commercials or video games using art and technology. The curriculum includes techniques in creating animation, motion graphics, modeling, character designing, compositing, etc. With the help of such programs such as Autodesk Maya, Adobe After Effects, and Blender students polish their design skills in animation and computer simulation, they also learn the technical side of storytelling and scene setting process.

Institutes Offering Animation and VFX Programs in Siliguri

  • Arena Animation Siliguri
  • MAAC
  • Moople – Institute of Animation and Design

Job Opportunities

  • Animator
  • VFX Artist
  • 3D Modeler
  • Motion Graphics Artist
  • Digital Media Specialist

Average Salary

₹3 – 12 lpa

Nursing

With the ever-growing population, nursing is an excellent chance for people who like to bring immediate improvement to individuals’ lives. It prepare the students to provide patient care, disease prevention and control, and perform clinical procedures that enable them to work with physicians and other healthcare professionals.

Institutes Offering Nursing Programs in Siliguri

  • North Bengal Medical College and Hospital (NBMCH)
  • Himalayan Institute of Nursing, Siliguri
  • Desun Nursing School
  • Anandaloke Institute of Nursing Education

Job Opportunities

  • Registered Nurse (RN)
  • Nurse Practitioner (NP)
  • Nurse Educator
  • Intensive Care Unit (ICU) Nurse
  • Pediatric Nurse

Average Salary

₹1.5- 10 lpa

Siliguri offers many educational opportunities for various career paths. Whether in management, healthcare, arts, or IT, the right training can pave the way for success. Research the available options, align them with your goals, and invest in continuous learning. Building a strong portfolio, networking, and developing both technical and soft skills will enhance your career prospects. With the right education and dedication, you can create a bright future in your chosen field.
