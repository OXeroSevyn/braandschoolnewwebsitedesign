List of Fashion Design Institutes in Siliguri

  • siliguri
Post By Mehek Verma
Published June 16, 2025

Fashion design is becoming a popular career choice among students in India, and it’s easy to see why. Fashion today is not just about style – it’s a creative field that also brings together technology and business. With India’s fashion industry expected to cross ₹8,000 crores by 2025, the need for skilled designers and stylists is growing fast – and cities like Siliguri are catching up too.

Even in smaller cities like ours, the growth is clear. According to data from the Apparel Made-Ups and Home Furnishing Sector Skill Council (AMHSSC), the demand for fashion and garment industry professionals in Tier-2 cities like Siliguri has grown by over 18% in the past three years. Locally, more boutiques and online clothing stores are opening, showing just how much the industry is expanding here.

This means fashion design is now a great career option, even in Siliguri. The present blog lists the top fashion design institutes in Siliguri, along with all the important details you may need, like courses, fee ranges, and why they stand out.

Inspiria Knowledge Campus

The fashion design and management course at Inspiria Knowledge Campus involves not just fashion design but also development and marketing, making it a complete industry package. Graduates are equipped to understand target markets, brand strategy, and business fundamentals alongside design. The institute also emphasizes entrepreneurship, helping students to eventually build their own labels or manage retail ventures.

Course Offered:
B.Sc. Fashion Design and Management (4 years)
Fee: Approx ₹1 lakh/year
Website: https://inspiria.edu.in/degree-courses/school-media/fashion-design/
Contact: 89007 55550
Email: contact@inspiria.edu.in
Address: Phase II, Himachal Vihar, Matigara, Siliguri

JD Institute of Fashion Technology

This institute is a part of India’s leading design education network with over 40+ campuses across India. JD Siliguri offers a broad and industry-focused curriculum. The course emphasizes CAD training along with Photoshop, Illustrator, InDesign, internships, and real-world opportunities to showcase their designs at the biggest fashion event by the institution, The Fashion Awards.

Courses Offered:

  • Diploma in Fashion Design (1 year)
  • Advanced Diploma in Fashion Design (2 years)
  • B.Voc in Fashion Design (3 years, in collaboration with SPU)
  • Certificate Courses (Fashion Styling, Pattern Making, etc., 3–6 months)
  • UG in Fashion Design (3 years)

Fee: Approx ₹2 lakh/year
Website: https://jdinstitute.ac.in/states/siliguri/
Contact:  9810031557
Email: siliguri@jdinstitute.co
Address: 4th Floor, Tradium Mall, Sevoke Rd, near Checkpost, Chayan Para, Don Bosco Colony, Siliguri

NIF Global Siliguri

NIF Global is another great institute in Siliguri which offers a mix of fashion, vocational, and retail management programs. The courses blend design with sustainability, luxury business insights, supply chains, and entrepreneurship, preparing students for roles in global and ethical fashion sectors with a focus on teaching everything right from the scratch like to design, manufacture and marketing.

Courses Offered:

  • B.Des in Fashion Design (4 years)
  • B.Voc in Fashion Design (3 years)
  • M.Voc in Fashion Design (2 years)

Fee: Approx ₹45k/year

Cadence Academy

Cadence Academy is another well-known institute in Siliguri which offers degree and diploma programs that cover both creative and technical aspects of fashion design, ideal for students aiming for either vocational training or full-fledged professional degrees.

Courses Offered:

  • 2-Year Advanced Diploma

International School of Design (INSD)

The fashion design courses at INSD are designed to cover everything from design principles to including textile knowledge, garment construction, and styling. Students also get exposure to reputed fashion brands, work on live assignments, and develop a strong foundation in pattern drafting, tailoring, and applying principles of fashion design.


Courses Offered:

  • 6-Month Certificate Programs
  • UG Fashion Design (3 years)
  • PG Fashion Design (2 years)
  • Advance Courses (1–2 years)
  • UG / PG Course- 4 years

IFM Academy Siliguri

IFM offers a compact, career-oriented curriculum combining technical skills, creativity, and business know-how. Their faculty consists of certified trainers, and the academy ensures feedback-based training with live projects, seminars, and workshops and placement assistance.

Courses Offered:

  • B.Sc in Fashion Design (3 years)
  • M.Sc in Fashion Design (2 years)
  • Diploma in Fashion Design (1 year)

Fashion design is one of the few career paths that’s genuinely skill-based and job-oriented. But, choosing the right course and institute can be tricky. So before you jump in, make sure to visit the campuses, ask about placements and internships, see what specialisations they offer and reach out to current students or alumni.

Take your time, compare your options, and pick the institute that best suit your goals.
