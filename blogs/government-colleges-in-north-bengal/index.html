Top Government Colleges in North Bengal

  • Blog
Post By braand
Published December 24, 2025

Many students in North Bengal want to study in government colleges because the fees is low and education is good. These colleges are trusted by students and parents for many years. In places like Siliguri, Darjeeling, Dinajpur, Jalpaiguri and Cooch Behar, there are some good government colleges where students from nearby towns and villages come to study.

This blog talks about some popular government colleges in North Bengal. We have included colleges from different districts so that students can get a basic idea before choosing. If you are confused about where to take admission, this list may help you a little.

Government Colleges in Jalpaiguri District


Ananda Chandra College

Ananda Chandra College, mostly called AC College, is one of the old government colleges in North Bengal. It is affiliated with North Bengal University and many students from nearby areas study here. The college has a big campus with green trees, open space and a calm environment which feels good for studying. The college offers many undergraduate courses in Arts and Science, and also has a postgraduate course in Bengali. There are labs, playground and basic facilities for students. AC College also supports student activities like NCC, NSS, seminars, cultural programs and different clubs. These activities help students gain confidence and learn teamwork along with studies.

Courses: B.A, B.Com and B.Sc
Fees: The cost of a first-year bachelor can range from 4870 to 13570 approx.

Website: https://www.accollege.in/

Address: Siristola Near Ac College, Jalpaiguri

Dhupguri Girl’s College

Dhupguri Girls College is meant mainly for girls who want to continue studies after school. Many students from Dhupguri and nearby areas come here. The college offers subjects in arts stream and helps girls build confidence through education. Classes are simple and teachers are supportive. The college environment feels safe and comfortable for girl students. For many families, this college is important because it allows daughters to study without going far from home.

Courses: B.A
Fees: The fees can range from 15,000 to 22,000 approx for a 4-Year Undergraduate degree.

Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College

Jalpaiguri Government Engineering College is known as JGEC. It is one of the old engineering colleges in North Bengal. Students come here from different parts of West Bengal to study engineering. The campus is large and has hostels, classrooms, and labs. Engineering students study hard here, and college life is quite busy. Many students prepare for future jobs while studying here. This college has a strong name in technical education in this region.

Maynaguri College

Maynaguri College is a government college located near Maynaguri town in Jalpaiguri district. The college is surrounded by open land and greenery, so the environment is quiet and good for studies. It was established in 1999 and is affiliated with the University of North Bengal. The college mainly offers undergraduate courses in Arts and Science for students from nearby areas. Facilities like classrooms, library and basic labs are available for students. Maynaguri College is helpful for students who want to continue higher education without going far from home and at an affordable cost.

P.D. Women’s College

Prasannadeb Women’s College, commonly called P.D. Women’s College, is one of the oldest women’s colleges in North Bengal, located in Jalpaiguri town. It was started in 1950 to give good higher education especially to women, and it is affiliated to the University of North Bengal. The college offers many courses in Arts, Science and even BBA and tourism subjects, with honours and general options so students can choose what they like. It also has activities like NSS, career guidance and special cells to support girls and women on campus. P.D. Women’s College works to make students confident, skilled and ready for today’s world while also focusing on women’s empowerment.

Government Colleges in Darjeeling District

Darjeeling Government College

Darjeeling Government College has been around since 1948 and is one of the older public colleges in the town. It offers a wider set of courses including science, arts, commerce and some postgraduate options. The college has served local students for decades and is well known in the district for being a government run college with reasonable fees. The college also conduct social awareness programs and provide National Cadet Corps (NCC) and National Social Service (NSS) training to interested students. Sports and hostel facilities are also available in the campus.

Courses Offered: B.A., B.Sc., B.Com, M.A., M.Sc and PHD
Affiliation: University of North Bengal
Fees: The total course fee ranges between Rs. 3,000 to 6,000.

Darjeeling Hills University

Darjeeling Hills University is a newer public university, set up in 2021 to serve higher education needs of the hills. It was established under state legislation and started operations in recent years. Formerly, it was known as Greenfield University. The university offers postgraduate degrees only in English, History, Mass Communication, Mathematics, Nepali and Political Science and is planning to launch postgraduate courses in Geography and Psychology in 2026.

Courses Offered: M.A. and M.Sc

Netaji Subhas Mahavidyalaya

Established in 1985, Netaji Subhas Mahavidyalaya stands as a bridge in the educational journey of students in the region. The college started with 6 teaching and 9 non-teaching staff and now has emerged as a higher educational institute. At present, college offers 4 years undergraduate programme courses. It offers undergraduate courses in Arts and Commerce. Situated in Cooch behar, the college is also known as Haldibari College, is a general degree college. With its commitment to academic excellence and an inclusive learning atmosphere, Netaji Subhash Mahavidyalaya continues to empower young minds and nurture future leaders in the region.

Courses: The college offers UG programs like BA, BCom and BSc

Dinhata College

Founded in 1956, Dinhata college is a Government-aided general degree college , one of the oldest colleges in Dinhata, West Bengal. The college was founded to bring higher education, especially for students of rural background and first-generation learners. Affiliated with Cooch Behar Panchana Brahma University and recognised under UGC, the college has spread across 16 acre campus, it boasts a large central library, separate girls and boys hostel, sports facilities and support services like career counselling and scholarships. The college offers a wide array of undergraduate courses in Arts, Science, and Commerce, with honours programmes. Dinhata college also encourages students to participate in extracurricular activities like NSS, NCC, seminars, awareness programs and many more, so the students can have a balanced learning environment.
