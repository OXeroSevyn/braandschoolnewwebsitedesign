Brands by Braand School Students

  • Updates
Post By Mehek Verma
Published April 30, 2025

Some brands start in garages, some in basements, and some over coffee shops. But at Braand School -they start in classrooms where students are given the tools, mentorship, and freedom to shape their ideas into real brands.

Our students don’t just study; they build their own brand while learning in our courses. Whether it’s a digital marketing agency, a jewelry brand, or a lifestyle product, these brands are more than just class projects— they’re real brands, by real students and the result of their hard work, determination, and skills developed at Braand School.

Why We Encourage Students to Build Their Own Brands?

  • To make students learn not just theory, but also apply what they learn through real projects.
  • They gain hands-on experience with real audiences and challenges in the market.
  • It helps them test their skills beyond theory.
  • Building something of their own, no matter how big or small, boosts their confidence.

As Zig Ziglar once said:
“You don’t have to be great to start, but you have to start to be great.”

And that’s exactly what our students do — they start. They try. They build. And they grow.

Here are some of the amazing businesses our students have created while learning:-

Pixelpact Design Studio

Pixelpact is a design studio providing digital illustrations, visual identity and graphics creation, and branding. Souvik, a talented design student, started with freelancing projects and then he rapidly developed into a brand owner providing modern and creative digital design and branding services.

Founder: Souvik Biswas, Branding Batch’25
Product/Service: Logo design, digital illustrations, visual identity creation, and branding
Target Audience: Local companies and startups
Social Media Profile: @pixelpact

Escape Tag

Nikhil, inspired by his passion of traveling and adventure, founded Escape Tag which is a travel and experience-based brand offering carefully chosen adventure excursions, hikes, and off-beat homestay tour packages across India. The brand emphasizes on responsible travel, cultural immersion, and eco- friendly travel practices, so that the tourists enjoy a, authentic and complete first-hand experience of local culture and destinations.

Founder: Nikhil Gurung, Digital Marketing Batch’25
Product/Service: Eco-friendly homestays, trekking and tour packages
Target Audience: Eco-conscious travelers and adventure seekers
Social Media Profile: @escapetag

Bigmarketting

Our student, Sayed, created Bigmarketting, a full-service digital marketing agency, providing services ranging from content creation, SEO, performance marketing, and social media strategy to help businesses succeed online.

Founder: Sayed Afridi, Digital Marketing Batch’25
Product/Service: Social media marketing, SEO, content creation and graphic design
Target Audience: Local businesses
Social Media Profile: @bigmarketting

The Northern Eye

Northern Eye is a media and entertainment company that shares local news updates, interviews, documentaries and creator-led content covering North Bengal and Sikkim. It brings local voices into the spotlight and is a growing hub for meaningful and regional content,

Founder: Rahul Chhetri, Branding Batch’25
Product/Service: News content, documentaries and interviews
Target Audience: Curious local audience of North Bengal and Sikkim
Social Media Profile: @thenortherneye

Digital Bloom

Nashreen, specialized in content marketing, social media management, and campaign design founded a digital marketing agency Digital Bloom that seeks to enable regional and local companies increase their online presence by means of innovative strategies. Digital Bloom focuses on offering all web-based services like designing and developing static and dynamic websites, corportate presentations, seo etc.

Founder: Nashreen Khatoon, Digital Marketing Batch’24
Product/Service: SEO, SEM, branding, web design and development, social media management and email marketing
Target Audience: Local and regional businesses
Social Media Profile: @digitalbloom

Hey Lets Meat

Our student, Suraj, founded HeyLetsMeat to provide good quality and fresh meat in Siliguri. The brand is committed to offering premium cuts, meats and marinated products while stressing freshness, cleanliness, and rich, flavorful taste.

Founder: Suraj Bhowal, Branding Batch’25
Product/Service: Meat products
Target Audience: Non-vegetarian households
Social Media Profile: @heyletsmeat

Kiki’s Store

Kiki’s Store is a small retail business selling crochet items, ranging from soft toys, bags, keychains to bouquets and many more. Everything is hand-made using good quality yarn and eco-friendly materials.

Founder: Anjal Rai, Branding Batch’25
Product/Service: Crochet products
Target Audience: Gift shoppers, home decor enthusiasts, eco-conscious buyers
Social Media Profile: @ki._.ki._.store

Aqua Plus

Regarding the Founding: Seeing a gap in the Siliguri drinking water delivery system, Sehbaz Ahmed started Aquaplus to provide high quality RO water cans to the doorstep. It offers subscription-based services for commercial buildings, households, hostels and events. It offers developed a tech-enabled fix.  His operations-first perspective guarantees effective client retention and service quality.

Founder: Shehbaaz Ahmed, Branding Batch’25
Product/Service: Potable water cans
Target Audience: Households, offices, hotels etc.
Social Media Profile: @aquaplus

Advertaa

Advertaa is a digital marketing agency, started by our student, Bapon, it goes beyond online marketing. It offers specialised digital marketing services to help clients stay ahead of their competition and also offline marketing solutions like hoardings, flex, printing and packaging, perfect for brands looking to grow both online and offline.

Founder: Bapon Biswas, Digital Marketing Batch’25
Product/Service: Website development, SEO, SMM, Branding, Content marketing, printing and packaging, hoardings, logo design and much more.
Target Audience: Local businesses
Social Media Profile: @advertaa

Bunnies Studio

Bunnies Studio is a small handmade jewelry brand started by Saru, that sells a collection of cute accessories, handmade gifts and jewelry- all curated with a personal and aesthetic touch.

Founder: Saru Chetri, Branding Batch’25
Product/Service: Hand-made jewelry and gifts
Target Audience: Teens and women
Social Media Profile: @bunniesstudio

SOL Travel

Sol Travel is a travel agency focusing on small group tours to Bhutan. It focuses on providing comprehensive local cultural experiences, customized itineraries, and environmentally friendly trips, with each trip is guided by Dipesh to ensure a memorable and authentic experience.

Founder: Dipesh Agarwal, Digital Marketing Batch’25
Product/Service: Customized small group tours to Bhutan
Target Audience: Tourists
Social Media Profile: @soltravel

Yarns & Yarner

Yarns and Yarner is a crochet brand offering a range of handmade items like charms, cardigans, scarves, cushions, beanies, and even flower bouquets, with every product crafted with high quality yard and care.

Founder: Sanjana Chhetri, Digital Marketing Batch’25
Product/Service: Crochet products
Target Audience: People who love handmade crochet items
Social Media Profile: @yarns&yarner

Buzz Builders

Buzz Builders is a performance-oriented digital marketing agency that focuses on increasing digital visibility, leads, and online sales.
Founder: Jeet Biswas, Branding Batch’25
Product/Service: SMM, SEM, Website development
Target Audience: Startups and businesses
Social Media Profile: @buzzbuilders111

Rohit Reads

Rohit, a passionate reader of self-help and historical fiction, built his personal brand “Rohit Reads” by sharing honest and thoughtful book recommendations. His consistent and engaging writing has attracted a community of like-minded readers who value depth, reflection, and meaningful stories.

Founder: Rohit Upadhya, Digital Marketing Batch’25
Product/Service: Book recommendations and reviews
Target Audience: Self-help and historical fiction readers
Social Media Profile: @rohitreads

Tripokidoki

Tripokidoki is a travel brand designed for young, adventurous people in their 20s and 30s who love exploring new places with like-minded people. Focused on Thailand and Vietnam, it offers group travel experiences that mix island hopping, safaris, café culture, and street shopping — all with a touch of fun, friendship, and unforgettable memories.

Founder: Rip Pandhi Lepcha, Digital Marketing Batch’24
Product/Service: Island hopping, safaris, café hopping, street shopping, and curated group tours
Target Audience: Young travelers aged 20–30 looking for exciting, social trips
Social Media Profile: @tripokidoki

It’s a proud moment for us to watch our students go from learners to entrepreneurs— from brainstorming ideas, experimenting, designing, to launching real products and services.

What brings them all together is how it all started — with a simple idea, a bit of courage, and a lot of curiosity.

To our students:
We’re proud of you.
Keep building — we’re cheering you on!

To those considering joining us:
Your brand story could be the next!
